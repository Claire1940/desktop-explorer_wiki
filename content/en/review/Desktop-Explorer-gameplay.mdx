Navigating the digital landscape of this retro psychological horror game requires a sharp eye and analytical thinking. The core **Desktop Explorer gameplay** centers around a simulated CRT monitor running a vintage 90s operating system.   <Step num="1" title="Boot the System and Map the Directories">

  <Step num="2" title="Analyze the Chat Archives">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Restore the Recycle Bin">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Decryption Program">
    Open the Decryption Tool on the desktop. Enter the password obtained from the chat archives to unlock the secure system folder. This action grants you administrator access to the hidden D: drive.
  </Step>
