<Section icon={Target} title="The Stalagmite Letters and Backtracking Sequence">

However, simply writing down these letters is not enough to solve the puzzle immediately. If you attempt to press onward past the stalagmites, you will quickly hit a dead end with no visible exit.

To trigger the next sequence, you must thoroughly inspect the dead-end wall. After spending a brief moment searching the crevices, a loud thunk sound will echo from far off behind the cave, indicating that the environment has shifted. You must now backtrack all the way to the cave's entrance to find the path forward.
