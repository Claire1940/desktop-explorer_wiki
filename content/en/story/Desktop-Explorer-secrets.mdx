<Section icon={Target} title="Solving the Hourglass Math Riddle">

The most difficult of the **Desktop Explorer secrets** is the Hourglass Room. Upon entering, the mirrors shatter, and you are trapped in a room filling with sand. You must solve a complex mathematical riddle based on a poem and a set of symbols before the time runs out.

The poem mentions several key symbols:
1. **Blue Moon**
2. **Red Eye**
3. **Bloodstained Feathers**
4. **Sapphire Scepters**

The critical clue is written on the wall: **"red face = 48"**.

| Symbol | Base Value | Multiplier / Context | Total Contribution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Red Face** | 4 | Hourglass Mark 12 | 48 (4 x 12) |
| **Blue Moon** | 1 | Mark 4 | 4 |
| **Red Eye** | 2 | Mark 9 | 18 |
| **Feathers** | 5 | Mark 7 | 35 |
| **Scepters** | 5 | Mark 2 | 10 |

The riddle requires you to sum these values based on their specific multipliers found in the room's environment. For example, if the Blue Moon rises while the Red Eye leaves, you must calculate the product of their values and the hourglass marks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze the Wall Clue">
    Observe the "red face = 48" text. Since the red face appears at the 12-hour mark, the base value of the face is 4.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Symbol Marks">
    Check the hourglass or the surrounding walls for marks associated with the Moon, Eye, Feathers, and Scepters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Perform the Calculation">
    Multiply each symbol's base value by its corresponding mark and sum them together.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enter the Password">
    The resulting number (e.g., 67) is the password to exit the room and stop the sandfall.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Calculation Verified">
In the current version of the game, the sum of 4 (Moon) + 18 (Eye) + 35 (Feathers) + 10 (Scepters) equals **67**. This is the most common solution for the Episode 3 hourglass.
</Callout>
